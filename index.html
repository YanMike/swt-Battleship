Play Battleship

It's time to play! Just press start.

Choose a position for each of your ships!

Therefor, just select 1-10 for horizontal (x-axis) and A-J for vertical (y-axis) position.

But be aware! Ships are allowed to touch, but not to overlap!
When you filled in all fields, the Done!-Button gets enabled and you can start playing!
In future releases there will be an option to choose the level of difficulty. At the moment, only the easiest level is implemented.

"My Troups" is the grid where you can see bot's shots.
Click on the "Battlefield" grid to shoot and find bot's ships.
